## **How to Create a Client at the Company Level**

Clients can be added either **manually** or using **bulk upload** for faster onboarding.

---

### **Access the Client Directory**

1. Go to the **Home Screen**.
2. On the **Top Navigation Bar**, hover over the **People** menu.
3. Click on **Directory** from the dropdown.
4. Navigate to the **"Clients"** tab.

---

### **Option 1: Create Client Manually**

1. Click the **"Create Client"** button.
2. A **right-side drawer** will open with a form.

### **Fill in Client Details**

Enter the following fields:

- **Client Name**
- **Email Address**
- **Phone Number**
- **Address Line 1**
- **Address Line 2**
- **City**
- **State**
- **Postal Code**
- **Country**

Click **Save** to add the client to the directory.

---

### **Option 2: Bulk Upload Clients**

1. Go to the **"Bulk Upload"** tab inside the client creation drawer.
2. Download the **Sample CSV file** provided at the bottom.
3. Fill in client details in the CSV using the exact column headers.

### Important Notes:

- **Do not modify or remove the column headers** in the CSV file.
- Changing headers can cause sync issues during upload.
1. Once completed, upload the filled CSV file.
2. Click **Upload** to add all clients in bulk.
