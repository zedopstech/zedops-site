## **How to Delete a Client at the Company Level**

You can remove a client from the directory in just a few simple steps.

---

### **Access the Client Directory**

1. Go to the **Home Screen**.
2. On the **Top Navigation Bar**, hover over the **People** menu.
3. Click on **Directory** from the dropdown.
4. Navigate to the **"Clients"** tab.

---

### **Delete an Existing Client**

1. Find the client you want to remove.
2. Click the **Delete icon** next to their name.
3. A **confirmation popup** will appear.

---

### **Confirm Deletion**

- Review the client's name and details.
- Click **Confirm** to permanently delete the client.

> ⚠️ Note: This action is irreversible. Please double-check before proceeding.
>

---

✅ The client will be removed from the directory instantly.
