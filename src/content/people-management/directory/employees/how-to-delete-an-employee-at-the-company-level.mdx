## **How to Delete an Employee at the Company Level**

You can easily remove an employee from the company directory by following these steps:

---

### **Access the Employee Directory**

1. Go to the **Home Screen**.
2. On the **Top Navigation Bar**, hover over the **People** menu.
3. Click on **Directory** from the dropdown.

---

### **Delete an Existing Employee**

1. On the Directory dashboard, open the **"Employees"** tab.
2. Use the search bar or scroll to find the employee you want to remove.
3. Click on the **Delete icon** next to the employee’s name.
4. A confirmation popup will appear.

---

### **Confirm Deletion**

- Review the employee name and details.
- Click **Confirm** to permanently delete the employee.

> ⚠️ Note: This action cannot be undone. Make sure you intend to remove the employee before confirming.
>

---

The employee will be removed from the company directory immediately.
