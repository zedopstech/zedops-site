## **How to Edit an Employee at the Company Level**

Follow these simple steps to update an existing employee’s details:

---

### **Access the Employee Directory**

1. Go to the **Home Screen**.
2. On the **Top Navigation Bar**, hover over the **People** menu.
3. Click on **Directory** from the dropdown.

---

### **Edit an Existing Employee**

1. On the Directory dashboard, go to the **"Employees"** tab.
2. Use the search bar or scroll to find the employee you want to edit.
3. Click on the **Edit icon** next to the employee’s name.
4. A right-side drawer will open with the employee’s existing information.

---

### **Update Details**

You can update the following fields:

- First Name & Last Name
- Email
- Phone Number
- Employee ID
- Job Title
- Classification
- Emergency Contact Information
1. Make the necessary changes and click **Save**.

---

That’s it! The employee’s profile will be updated in the company directory.
