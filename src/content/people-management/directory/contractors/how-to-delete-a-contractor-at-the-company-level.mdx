## **How to Delete a Contractor at the Company Level**

You can remove a contractor from the directory in a few quick steps.

---

### **Access the Contractor Directory**

1. Go to the **Home Screen**.
2. On the **Top Navigation Bar**, hover over the **People** menu.
3. Click on **Directory** from the dropdown.
4. Navigate to the **"Contractors"** tab.

---

### **Delete an Existing Contractor**

1. Find the contractor you want to remove.
2. Click the **Delete icon** next to their name.
3. A **confirmation popup** will appear.

---

### **Confirm Deletion**

- Review the contractor’s name and details.
- Click **Confirm** to permanently delete the contractor.

> ⚠️ Note: This action cannot be undone. Ensure you want to proceed before confirming.
>

---

✅ The contractor will be removed from the directory immediately.
