## **How to Create a Contractor at the Company Level**

Contractors can be added either **manually** or through a **bulk upload** for faster onboarding.

---

### **Access the Contractor Directory**

1. Go to the **Home Screen**.
2. On the **Top Navigation Bar**, hover over the **People** menu.
3. Click on **Directory** from the dropdown.
4. Navigate to the **"Contractors"** tab.

---

### **Option 1: Create Contractor Manually**

1. Click the **"Create Contractor"** button.
2. A **right-side drawer** will open with a form.

### **Fill in Contractor Details**

Enter the following fields:

- **Company Name**
- **Email Address**
- **Phone Number**
- **Address**
- **Industry**
- **Website**

Click **Save** to add the contractor to the directory.

---

### **Option 2: Bulk Upload Contractors**

1. Go to the **"Bulk Upload"** tab inside the contractor creation drawer.
2. Download the **Sample CSV file** provided at the bottom.
3. Fill in contractor details in the file.

### ⚠️ Important Notes:

- **Do not edit or remove column headers** in the CSV.
- Incorrect headers will lead to sync errors during upload.
1. Upload the filled CSV file.
2. Click **Upload** to bulk-add contractors.

---

✅ Contractors will now appear in the directory and can be managed as needed.
