## **How to Edit a Contractor at the Company Level**

You can easily update contractor details through the Contractor Directory.

---

### **Access the Contractor Directory**

1. Go to the **Home Screen**.
2. On the **Top Navigation Bar**, hover over the **People** menu.
3. Click on **Directory** from the dropdown.
4. Select the **"Contractors"** tab.

---

### **Edit an Existing Contractor**

1. Locate the contractor you want to update.
2. Click the **Edit icon** next to their name.
3. A **right-side drawer** will open with the contractor’s current details.

---

### **Update Contractor Information**

You can update the following fields:

- **Company Name**
- **Email Address**
- **Phone Number**
- **Address**
- **Industry**
- **Website**

Click **Save** after making the changes.

---

✅ The contractor's information will be updated in the system.
