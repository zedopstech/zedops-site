## **How to Stop a Time Card**

Once your work is complete, stop the time card to log your hours accurately.

---

### **Stop an Active Time Card**

1. Go to the **Time Card Dashboard**.
2. Find the **active** time card (running or resumed).
3. Click on the time card to open the **right-side drawer**.
4. Click the **"Stop Time Card"** button.

---

### **What Happens Next**

- The **Clock-Out Time** is automatically set to the current time.
- Your **total work duration** is calculated based on Clock-In and Clock-Out.
- The time card entry is now marked as **completed** and saved in the records.
