Follow these steps to create a new role and assign permissions:

1. **Go to Home**

   Start from the Home screen of the application.

2. **Navigate to Roles & Permissions**
    - Hover over the **People** tab in the top navigation bar.
    - Click on **Roles & Permission** from the dropdown.
3. **Create a New Role**
    - Click the **Create Role** button.
4. **Fill in Role Details**
    - **Role Name**: Enter a name for the new role.
    - **Status**: Set the status to **Active** to enable the role.
    - **Description**: Add a brief description of the role.
5. **Assign Permissions**
    - Choose the relevant permissions for the required modules by selecting the appropriate radio buttons.
6. **Save the Role**
    - Click the **Save** button to complete the setup.

Your new role is now created and ready to use.
