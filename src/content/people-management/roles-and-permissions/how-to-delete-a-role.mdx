Follow these steps to permanently remove a role:

1. **Go to Home**

   Start from the Home screen of the application.

2. **Navigate to Roles & Permissions**
    - Hover over the **People** tab in the top navigation bar.
    - Click on **Roles & Permission** from the dropdown.
3. **Find the Role to Delete**
    - Browse the list and locate the role you want to delete.
4. **Delete the Role**
    - Click the **Delete** icon next to the role.
    - Confirm the action when prompted to permanently remove the role.
