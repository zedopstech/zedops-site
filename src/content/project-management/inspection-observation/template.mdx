## **How to Configure Inspection Templates at the Project Level**

Inspection templates help standardize inspection forms across projects. You can create, assign, and manage templates from the **Inspection & Observation** settings.

---

### **Step 1: Access the Inspection Template Settings**

1. From the **Home Page**, go to the **Top Navigation Bar**.
2. Hover over **Project Management** → click on **Inspection & Observation**.
3. You’ll see a **list of inspections**.
4. Click on the **"Configure Inspection Template"** button.
5. You’ll be redirected to the **Inspection Template Settings** page.

---

### **Step 2: Create a New Inspection Template**

1. On the settings page, click **"+ Create Inspection Template"**.
2. You’ll be redirected to the **Template Builder** page.

### **Fill in Template Details**

- **Template Name** – Give your template a meaningful name.
- **Template Description** – Add a brief overview (optional but recommended).
- **Template Type** – Select the category or type of inspection (e.g., Safety, Quality).

---

### **Step 3: Add Inspection Items**

Use the table form to build your inspection checklist:

- **Title** – (Optional) Add the title of the inspection item.
- **Description** – (Optional) Add more details or instructions.
- **Response Type** – **Select** the type of input expected (e.g., Yes/No, Text, Dropdown, etc.).
- **Attachment Required** – Toggle whether the item requires file or photo uploads.
- Click **"+ Add Item"** to add more rows and build your checklist.

---

### **Step 4: Preview and Save**

- Use the **"Preview"** option at the top to see how the form will appear to end users.
- Once done, click **"Save"** to finalize your inspection template.

---

### **Step 5: Assign Template to Projects**

1. On the template list, locate the template you just created.
2. Under the **Project** column (e.g., `0/10`), click the count.
3. A **right-side drawer** will appear with a list of projects.
4. Select the projects you want this inspection template to be available for.
5. Click **Save**.

✅ Your inspection template is now assigned to the selected projects and ready for use.

---

### ⚙️ **Manage Existing Templates**

- **Edit**: Update the template details or items if needed.
- **Enable/Disable**: Control template visibility per project by toggling them on or off.
