## **How to Manage Materials in a Daily Log**

Use the **Materials** section in the Daily Log to track site deliveries and material usage accurately.

---

### **Accessing the Daily Log**

You can open the Daily Log in two ways:

### **Option 1: From Top Navigation**

- Click on **Daily Log** in the top navigation bar.
- A **left-side drawer** will appear with a list of projects.
- Select the desired **project name** to access the **Daily Log Dashboard**.

### **Option 2: From Project Management**

- Hover over the **Project Management** menu and click on **Manage Projects**.
- On the **Projects Dashboard**, click the **project name** or **Eye (üëÅÔ∏è) icon**.
- In the **Project Details Page**, click on the **"Daily Log" tab**.

### **Manage Materials in the Daily Log**

1. On the **Daily Log Details Page**, click on the **"Materials"** tab from the sidebar.

---

### **Log Delivered Materials**

1. Under the **Material Delivered** section, click the **"+ Add"** button.
2. A **right-side drawer** will open with a form.

- **Delivered By** ‚Äì Name of the person or vendor who delivered the materials.

### **Log Material Used**

1. Scroll down to the **Material Used** section.
2. Click the **"+ Add"** button to log materials used on-site.
3. A **drawer form** will open.

### **Used Materials Form Fields**

- **Material Name** ‚Äì Select the material consumed during the workday.
- **Unit of Measure (UOM)** ‚Äì Choose the relevant unit.
- **Used Quantity** ‚Äì Enter how much was used.
