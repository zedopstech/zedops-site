## **How to Create a New Daily Log**

Daily logs help capture on-site progress, activities, and notes for each project day.

---

### **Accessing the Daily Log**

You can access the Daily Log in two ways:

### **Option 1: From Top Navigation**

1. Click on **Daily Log** in the **Top Navigation Bar**.
2. A **left-side drawer** will appear with a list of projects.
3. Click on the desired **project name** ‚Äî you'll be redirected to the **Daily Log Dashboard** for that project.

### **Option 2: From Project Management**

1. Hover over the **Project Management** menu in the top navigation.
2. Click on **Manage Projects** ‚Äî this takes you to the **Projects Dashboard**.
3. Find the desired project and click either the **Project Name** or the **Eye (üëÅÔ∏è) icon**.
4. You‚Äôll land on the **Project Details** page.
5. Click on the **"Daily Log" tab** ‚Äî this opens the **Daily Log Dashboard**.

### **Create a New Daily Log**

1. On the **Daily Log Dashboard**, click the **"Create Daily Log"** button.
2. A **right-side drawer** will open.

### **Fill in Daily Log Details**

- **Arrival Date**: Select the date the team arrived on site.
- **Arrival Time**: Select the arrival time.

Click **"Create Daily Log"** to save.
